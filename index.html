Yogyakarta

Yogyakarta together with twin city Surakarta (Solo), is the cradle of
civilation on Java. This city was the seat of power that produced the
magnificent temples Borobudur and Prambanan in the 8th and 9th century
and the new powerful Mataram kingdom of the 16th and 17th century
